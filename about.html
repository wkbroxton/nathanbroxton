Nathan, a vibrant high schooler, seamlessly combines his academic pursuits with his undeniable talent in the arts. As an actor, he harbors a deep-rooted passion for crafting stories, diving deep into the worlds of video games, and captivating audiences with his singing.

His dedication shines in notable performances like "The Little Mermaid Jr." and as Travis in "A Raisin in the Sun” at the South Coast Repertory. He currently stars as a series regular, Jinx, on Nickelodeon's The Thundermans: Undercover.
